## Understanding Quotas

### Project Limits

Projects are saved height comparison charts. Each project stores:
- Characters in the comparison
- Chart settings and styling
- Background preferences
- Title and metadata

**What happens when you reach the limit:**
- You'll see a warning message
- You must delete old projects or upgrade
- Existing projects remain accessible

### Custom Character Limits

Custom characters are personally uploaded character images with custom heights.

**What happens when you reach the limit:**
- You can't create new custom characters
- Existing characters remain usable
- Delete unused characters or upgrade to continue

### Upload Size Limits

This is the maximum file size per image upload for custom characters.

**Recommendations:**
- Compress images before uploading
- Use PNG format for transparency
- Optimize images to stay under limits

### Gallery Submissions

Submit your best comparisons to the public gallery (coming soon) for others to discover.

**Submission Guidelines:**
- High-quality comparisons only
- Original or properly attributed content
- No inappropriate content
- Follow community guidelines

### Changing Plans

#### Upgrade

When you upgrade:
1. Your new limits take effect immediately
2. You're charged a prorated amount for the current period
3. Your next billing date stays the same

#### Downgrade

When you downgrade:
1. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period
2. You keep premium features until then
3. Make sure your usage fits within new limits before downgrade

### Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription anytime:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Subscription**
2. Click **"Manage Billing"**
3. Select **"Cancel Subscription"**
4. Confirm cancellation

**What happens after cancellation:**
- You keep paid features until the end of your billing period
- Then you're moved to the Free plan
- All your content remains accessible
- You may need to delete projects/characters to fit Free plan limits

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try premium features before subscribing?

Yes! Create a free account to try the core features. For premium features, we recommend starting with the Starter plan which you can cancel anytime.

### What happens to my projects if I downgrade?

Your projects remain accessible, but you may need to delete some to fit within your new plan's limits. You'll receive warnings before downgrade takes effect.

### Can I change from monthly to annual billing?

Yes! Contact support or cancel your current plan and resubscribe with annual billing to get the 20% discount.

### Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Currently, we don't offer free trials for paid plans. However, you can start with the Free plan to test the platform, then upgrade when ready.

### Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're not satisfied. Contact support with your reason and we'll process your refund.

### Do you offer student or educational discounts?

Yes! Educational institutions and verified students can contact us for special pricing. Email support with proof of enrollment or institution details.

### What payment information do you store?

We use secure payment processors (Stripe) and never store your full credit card details. Only the last 4 digits and expiration date are visible to you.

### Can I pause my subscription?

We don't offer subscription pausing, but you can cancel and resubscribe anytime without losing your data (within reasonable storage periods).
