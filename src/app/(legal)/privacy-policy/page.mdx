# Privacy Policy for NanoEdit

## Overview

NanoEdit ("we", "our", "us") operates the AI image editing platform and related services available at [https://compareheights.org](https://compareheights.org) (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you explore templates, upload images, submit prompts, or purchase credits described on our landing page.

We process personal data in accordance with applicable privacy laws. By using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

## Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to deliver the features highlighted on our landing experience, including:

1. **Account Details**
   - *What we collect*: Name, email address, locale, authentication provider IDs, avatar, and basic profile data when you create or sign in to an account.
   - *Why we collect it*: To authenticate users, personalize the dashboard, and provide customer support.

2. **Usage and Content Data**
   - *What we collect*: Prompts, uploaded images, generated outputs, task statuses, credit balances, feature interactions, and log data related to nano banana ai and Gemini model usage.
   - *Why we collect it*: To process editing tasks, display history in the console, improve quality, troubleshoot issues, and prevent abuse.

3. **Device and Technical Data**
   - *What we collect*: Device type, operating system, browser information, IP address, locale settings, and cookie identifiers.
   - *Why we collect it*: To secure accounts, localize the interface, analyze performance, and detect anomalies.

4. **Payment Information**
   - *What we collect*: Payment method details (handled by our processors), billing address, transaction identifiers, and plan selections.
   - *Why we collect it*: To process credit purchases or subscriptions, issue invoices, and comply with financial regulations.

5. **Communications**
   - *What we collect*: Messages sent to support, survey responses, and feedback about templates or outputs.
   - *Why we collect it*: To address requests, refine product features, and maintain service quality.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar tools to remember session state, authenticate requests, measure usage of features like the style gallery, and understand navigation patterns. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser, but disabling cookies may impact functionality.

## How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

- Provide and maintain AI editing workflows, credit accounting, and console insights.
- Personalize prompts, templates, and locale-specific content.
- Monitor system performance, detect fraud or misuse, and enforce Terms of Service.
- Communicate about feature updates, billing notices, or support responses.
- Analyze aggregated trends to enhance output quality, latency, and safety.

We do not sell personal information.

## Sharing with Third Parties

We may share information with:

- **Service Providers**: Hosting, analytics, payment processors, authentication, and AI infrastructure partners (including nano banana ai and Google Gemini) who help deliver the Service under confidentiality obligations.
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may transfer to the new owner subject to this Policy.

## Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to operate the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account data, subject to legitimate retention requirements.

## Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal information, including encrypted connections, access controls, and routine monitoring. While we strive for robust protection, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## International Data Transfers

Information may be processed and stored in locations where we or our providers operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards when transferring data across borders.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Submit requests via [support@compareheights.org](mailto:support@compareheights.org). We may verify your identity before responding.

You can manage marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions in emails or contacting us directly.

## Children’s Privacy

NanoEdit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us for removal.
