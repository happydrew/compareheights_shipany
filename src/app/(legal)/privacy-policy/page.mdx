# Privacy Policy for CompareHeights

## Overview

CompareHeights ("we", "our", "us") operates the height comparison tool and related services available at [https://compareheights.org](https://compareheights.org) (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our height comparison tool, create comparison charts, save projects, or use other features described on our platform.

We process personal data in accordance with applicable privacy laws. By using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

## Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to deliver the features of our height comparison platform, including:

1. **Account Details**
   - *What we collect*: Name, email address, locale, authentication provider IDs, avatar, and basic profile data when you create or sign in to an account.
   - *Why we collect it*: To authenticate users, save your height comparison projects, and provide customer support.

2. **Usage and Content Data**
   - *What we collect*: Height comparison projects, uploaded custom character images, saved charts, project settings, character selections, and usage statistics.
   - *Why we collect it*: To save and display your projects, enable sharing features, improve the platform, troubleshoot issues, and prevent abuse.

3. **Device and Technical Data**
   - *What we collect*: Device type, operating system, browser information, IP address, locale settings, and cookie identifiers.
   - *Why we collect it*: To secure accounts, optimize the interface for different devices, analyze performance, and detect anomalies.

4. **Payment Information** (if applicable)
   - *What we collect*: Payment method details (handled by our processors), billing address, transaction identifiers, and subscription selections.
   - *Why we collect it*: To process premium subscriptions or purchases, issue invoices, and comply with financial regulations.

5. **Communications**
   - *What we collect*: Messages sent to support, survey responses, and feedback about features or user experience.
   - *Why we collect it*: To address requests, refine product features, and maintain service quality.
